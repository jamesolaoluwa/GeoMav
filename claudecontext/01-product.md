# GeoMav — Product Context

Last updated: 2026-03-12

## What is GeoMav

GeoMav is an AI visibility monitoring platform that tracks how AI assistants (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Bing AI) represent businesses. It helps businesses monitor their mentions, detect hallucinations (incorrect claims), and optimize their visibility in AI-powered discovery.

## Three Core Capabilities

### 1. Monitor
Track AI visibility across platforms. GeoMav uses official APIs to submit realistic shopping queries relevant to the business's category and location on a weekly cycle. For each response, it extracts whether the business appeared, what claims were made, and what sources the AI relied on. The result is a dashboard showing AI Mention Rate, Claim Accuracy, and Source Attribution.

### 2. Validate
Every factual claim an AI makes about the business is automatically extracted and compared against a verified Business Profile. Claims are classified as:
- **Verified**: matches the profile
- **Outdated**: was once true but has changed
- **Fabricated**: has no basis in reality
- **Misleading**: technically true but presented deceptively

Fabricated claims trigger immediate alerts. Outdated claims trigger correction workflows.

### 3. Optimize
When monitoring reveals errors or gaps, GeoMav generates fixes: enriched JSON-LD structured data, optimized FAQ sections matching AI query patterns, and corrected business descriptions formatted for AI readability. After every correction, GeoMav re-queries AI platforms and logs before-and-after comparisons.
